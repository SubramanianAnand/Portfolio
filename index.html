About me

I'm Subramanian and I'm a

I’m a Full Stack Developer passionate about building scalable, secure, and user-friendly digital solutions. With experience across web, mobile, and cloud platforms, I love turning complex ideas into clean, efficient applications. On the frontend, I work with React.js, TailwindCSS, Bootstrap, and MaterialUI to create intuitive and modern interfaces. On the backend, I use FastAPI, Node.js, and REST APIs to deliver robust and reliable systems. I also develop mobile apps with React Native, Expo, and Flutter, and manage data with SQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, MinIO S3, Trino, and PyIceberg. My DevOps experience spans Docker, OpenShift, and observability tools like OTel, Prometheus, and Grafana—ensuring apps are not just functional, but also scalable, secure, and resilient. I’m especially interested in role-based access control (RBAC), data mesh principles, and observability-driven development—because I believe great software isn’t just about building features, but about building systems that last and adapt. When I’m not coding, I’m usually exploring AI/ML, data science, or automation projects—always curious about how technology can simplify and empower everyday life.
